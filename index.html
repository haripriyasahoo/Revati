Current Research

Exploring sustainable solutions through Environmental Biotechnology, Bioenergy, and waste management systems.

Biofuels

Algal Biofuel Production

This research focuses on optimizing the cultivation of microalgae for high-yield lipid production. By manipulating environmental stress factors such as nitrogen deprivation and light intensity, we aim to enhance the accumulation of triacylglycerols (TAGs) suitable for biodiesel conversion.

Sustainability

Industrial Bioremediation

Investigating the use of microbial consortia to degrade persistent organic pollutants (POPs) in industrial effluents. This study isolates specific bacterial strains capable of breaking down heavy metals and dyes from textile wastewater.

Green Energy

Microbial Fuel Cells

This project explores the generation of bioelectricity using Microbial Fuel Cells (MFCs) fueled by organic waste. We are testing novel electrode materials to improve electron transfer efficiency and power density.

Genetics

CRISPR in Agriculture

Analyzing the efficacy of CRISPR-Cas9 gene editing technology in improving crop resistance to drought and salinity. This literature review and meta-analysis evaluates the ethical considerations and regulatory landscape.
